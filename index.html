SIGCHI Korea Local Chapter is the Korea regionâ€™s chapter of the ACM SIGCHI, an interdisciplinary academic and professional group interested in human-computer interaction, user experience, usability, and other related disciplines. ACM SIGCHI organizes leading HCI conferences including ACM CHI, Ubicomp, UIST, and CSCW. Recently, the number of papers from Korea in major SIGCHI conferences has significantly increased due to enormous efforts of researchers in Korea.

Our mission is to strengthen the HCI community in Korea by fostering cross-disciplinary collaboration, increasing international visibility, and promoting SIGCHI initiatives. We provide networking opportunities and professional development support for junior researchers, helping to cultivate the next generation of HCI scholars. Through these efforts, we enhance the global impact of Korean HCI research while building a more connected and vibrant local community.

We are actively recruiting local chapter members and are like to receive your feedback on local chapter activities. Feel free to contact chapter officers!
